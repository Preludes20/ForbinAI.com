The Colossus Framework™

An independent AI governance framework designed to help organizations measure, monitor, and improve trust in artificial intelligence systems.

Built around practical principles for transparency, accountability, safety, privacy, and meaningful human oversight.

VIEW FRAMEWORK RELEASE v0.9
Mission

Responsible AI Requires Measurable Trust

The Colossus Framework™ exists to help organizations evaluate how AI systems are governed, how risks are managed, and how human authority is preserved as AI capabilities evolve.

The Five Core Domains

The Foundation of Responsible AI

The framework is organized around five interconnected domains.

H

Human Oversight

Preserves human judgment, authority, and accountable decision-making.

T

Transparency

Measures clarity, explainability, uncertainty, and openness.

A

Accountability

Assesses responsibility, traceability, and governance structures.

S

Safety

Evaluates harm, misuse, unsafe reliance, and unintended consequences.

P

Privacy

Protects personal information, autonomy, security, and human dignity.

Our Methodology

A Structured, Repeatable Approach

1. DEFINE

Define the AI system, use case, and evaluation scope.

2. ASSESS

Evaluate governance, oversight, evidence, and risk conditions.

3. MEASURE

Develop Colossus Risk Indicators™ for measurable trust conditions.

4. IMPROVE

Provide recommendations for stronger AI governance.

5. MONITOR

Track how trust, risk, and oversight change over time.
