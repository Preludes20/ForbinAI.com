The Colossus
Framework™

Organizations are deploying AI faster than governance frameworks can keep pace.

The Colossus Framework™ provides a structured approach for evaluating trust, managing risk, strengthening human oversight, and maintaining accountability in AI-enabled systems.

Supporting components, including the Colossus Safety Principles™ and the Colossus Risk Indicators Framework™, provide structured guidance for assessing AI safety, emerging risks, governance maturity, and organizational readiness.

WHY COLOSSUS™

The Colossus Framework™ is designed to help organizations govern AI with confidence, transparency, managing risk, sustaining accountability, and preserving meaningful human oversight.

Independent

Evaluate AI systems using objective, evidence-based criteria rather than relying on vendor claims, marketing assurances, or unverified performance assertions.

Human-Centered

Keep human judgment, oversight, and accountability at the center of AI-enabled decision-making.

Risk-Informed

Identify governance gaps, emerging risks, and areas requiring additional safeguards before they become critical AI issues.

Actionable

Transform AI assessments into practical findings and recommendations that help organizations strengthen trust, governance, and accountability.

Continuous

Support ongoing monitoring as AI systems evolve, ensuring governance keeps pace with technological change.

The Five Colossus Principles™

The Foundation of Responsible AI

The Colossus Framework™ is built on five foundational principles that guide responsible AI governance and trustworthy outcomes.

Safe & Reliable

Evaluates potential for harm, misuse, and performance failures to ensure AI systems operate safely under real-world conditions.

Human-Centered

Examines the role of human judgment, oversight, and control in AI-enabled decision-making.

Fair & Equitable

Assesses AI systems for bias, discrimination, and equitable treatment across all users and groups.

Transparent

Measures clarity, explainability, and openness of data, processes, and model behavior.

Accountable

Assesses responsibility, traceability, and governance structures that ensure clear ownership of AI outcomes.

Our Methodology

A Structured, Repeatable Approach

1. DEFINE

Define the AI system, use case, stakeholders, and Colossus evaluation scope.

2. ASSESS

Evaluate the system against the Colossus Framework™ principles: Safety, Transparency, Accountability, Fairness, and Oversight.

3. SCORE

Generate Colossus risk scores with evidence-based quantitative and qualitative findings.

4. RECOMMEND

Provide Colossus-aligned recommendations to reduce risk and strengthen governance.

5. MONITOR

Continuously monitor compliance with the Colossus Framework™, identifying emerging risks and changes requiring reassessment.
