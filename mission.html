Our Mission

Mission Statement

FORBINAI™

ForbinAI exists to ensure that artificial intelligence remains transparent, accountable, and aligned with human interests first and foremost.

As artificial intelligence systems become increasingly powerful and autonomous, individuals, organizations, and human institutions require independent frameworks to evaluate AI trustworthiness, risk, oversight, and real-world impact.

ForbinAI develops governance frameworks, methodologies, and evaluation tools that promote transparency, accountability, safety, privacy, and responsible innovation in artificial intelligence while enabling informed decision-making about its adoption and use.

We believe artificial intelligence should augment human capability, judgment, and progress—but never replace human authority, responsibility, accountability, or meaningful control.
